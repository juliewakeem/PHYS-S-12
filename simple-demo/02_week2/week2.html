I was thinking that I should start relatively easy so that I can understand the controls better. I used the caliphers in the lab to take in the thickness of the object as well as the length and the width. The width and length of the coffee cup holder was about 100.44 mm. The thickness was 4.76 mm.</p2> First, I created the shape of the coffee cup holder which was a square. ![image of embroidery](Week2image4.jpg) Then, I extruded the object and put in the thickness. I noticed that the corners of the coffee cup holder was a little more round than straight edged so I applied a fillet of 1 to mimic the curved corners. ![image of embroidery](Week2image5.jpg) Lastly, I applied the fillet to all the corners and the model was done. ![image of embroidery](Week2image6.jpg)
